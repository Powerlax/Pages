Deepfake Detection Research

I have recently finished my most recent research project, titled "Unveiling Truth Through Gestures: A Multimodal Deepfake Detection System," where I use multimodal information such as gestures and artifacts to detect a deepfake. This is currently in the process of being published in the Princeton Journal of Interdisciplinary Research and has been accepted to the international ALLEGET conference.

Visually Impaired Aid

A while back, I worked on a computer vision based system to make street crossings safer for the visually impaired by building a device that used object detection and depth estimation to detect if any oncoming traffic would hit a visually impaired person. This paper won 5th in the state at the Washington JSHS conference. I also got the oppurunity to present this at the international level. You can read the paper here.
